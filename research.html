Research

Research Experiences

Ongkeko Lab, Researcher

Details
  • Conduct a systematic meta-analysis from 68 studies on Covid-19 outcome disparity based on racial and socioeconomic factors. Personal contribution includes acquiring, cataloging and data extraction from 21,745 papers in academic databases, creating and executing R programs for Risk Ratio, multi-variable regression analysis
  • Align microbial reads on tumor tissue sequencing from Head and Neck Squamous Cell Carcinoma (HNSCC) and Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC) patients using Pathoscope; perform downstream analysis including Kruskal Wallis differential expression, Kaplan Meier survival and Gene Set Enrichment Analysis (GSEA) to discover potential correlational mechanism on pathways and mutations
  • Train, fine-tune and test Light Gradient Boosting Machine (LGBM) machine learning algorithm on circular RNA expression for HNSCC diagnosis and prognosis prediction

CTEPH Nuclear Medical Image Analysis, Researcher

Details
  • Collaborated with 5 team members at the First Affiliated Hospital of Guangzhou Medical University under the leadership of a nuclear medical imaging specialist and a data scientist to construct and analyzes 3D imaging of computed tomography (CT) and single-photon emission computed tomography (SPECT) to achieve
    Following objectives:
    • Quantify pulmonary blood volume (PBV) by developing a visual scoring system
    • Diagnose chronic thromboembolic pulmonary hypertension (CTEPH)
    • Assess the effectiveness of balloon pulmonary angioplasty (BPA) as treatment
    Individual contributions:
    • Set up environment in cloud server, implement core-code into program and batch process the scans
    • Create an interactive user interface for easy execution and automatically format results into a human-readable report
